Detailed Description

Designed to control the simple aircraft model. Horizontal channel controlled through roll rate or heading rate Vertical channel controll through altitude or glide slope (tan(z_vel / sqrt(x_vel^2 + y_vel^2))) Note that pitch for the simple aircraft model is actually in the direction of the velocity vector in the vertical plane (angle of attack assumed to be zero) Speed controlled through a separate channel. A glider can be controlled through here if the speed is always set to match the glide speed based on the roll and pitch angles.
